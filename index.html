  • Homing Soulmass: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim after giving him Logan's Scroll
  • Homing Crystal Soulmass: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 5000 souls after giving him the Crystal ScrollCrystal Scroll

  • Soul Spear: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 5000 souls after giving him Logan's Scroll
  • Crystal Soul Spear: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 15,000 souls after giving him the Crystal Scroll
  • White Dragon Breath: Transpose Soul of Consumed Oceiros
  • Soul Stream: Found in Lothric Castle in the Grand Archive behind a hidden room with a Boreal Knight
  • Soul Greatsword: Sold by Yoel/Yuria of Londor and Orbeck of Vinheim for 5000 souls
  • Farron Flashsword: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 3000 souls
  • Magic Weapon: Sold by Yoel/Yuria of Londor for 4500 souls and Orbeck of Vinheim for 3000 souls
  • Great Magic Weapon: From the Farron Keep Perimeter bonfire, turn around 180 and head towards the shortcut gate to Road of Sacrifices. The spell will be on a corpse at the gate
  • Crystal Magic Weapon: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 10,000 souls after giving him the Crystal Scroll
  • Magic Shield: Sold by Yoel/Yuria of Londor for 4500 souls and Orbeck of Vinheim for 3000 souls
  • Great Magic Shield: Found in Irythyll Dungeon
  • Spook: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 2000 souls
  • Aural Decoy: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 2000 souls
  • Pestilent Mercury: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 1000 souls
  • Cast Light: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 1000 souls after giving him the Golden Scroll
  • Repair: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 2000 souls after giving him the Golden Scroll
  • Hidden Weapon: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 1500 souls after giving him the Golden Scroll
  • Hidden Body: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 3000 souls after giving him the Golden Scroll
  • Chameleon: Found in the Church of Yorshka bonfire in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley during Anri's questline. The spell will drop from an assassin disguised as a statue. Otherwise found in Darkmoon Tomb from Anri's corpse.
  • Twisted Wall of Light: Sold by Orbeck of Vinheim for 6000 souls after giving him the Golden Scroll
  • Deep Soul: Transpose Soul of Deacon of the Deep
  • Great Deep Soul: Transpose the Soul of the Deacons of the Deep
  • Affinity: Sold by Karla for 15,000 souls
  • Dark Edge: Sold by Karla for 8000 souls

  • Gnaw: Purchased form Irina of Carim for 2000 souls
  • Dorhy's Gnawing: Guaranteed kill reward for the screaming Evangelist in Irithyll of the Boreal Valley
  • Lifehunt Scythe: Transpose Soul of Aldrich

Master of Expression (Learn all gestures)

  • Point Forward: Automatically received
  • Point Up: Automatically received
  • Point Down: Automatically received
  • Wave: Automatically received
  • Beckon: Talk to Yoel of Londor not far from the Foot of the High Wall bonfire.
  • Call Over: Received after your trade a homeward bone with Pickle Pee, Pump-a-Rum Crow
  • Welcome: Talk to the Cornyx in the Undead Settlement, and accept his services. Talk to him again at Firelink Shrine to get the gesture
  • Applause: Talk to Leonhard in Firelink Shrine after killing the imprisoned Darkwraith
  • Quiet Resolve: Finish Anri's questline up to Irithyll of the Boreal Valley. They will grant you the gesture when speaking to them at the Church of Yorshka Bonfire.
  • Jump for Joy: Automatically received
  • Joy: Automatically received
  • Rejoice: Received during the Siegward questline.
  • Hurrah!: Talk to Blacksmith Andre
  • Praise the Sun: Received from praying at the Altar of Sunlight in Lothric Castle
  • My Thanks!: Obtained from Knight Slayer Tsorig deep in the Demon Ruins
  • Bow: Automatically received
  • Proper Bow: Kill Yellowfinger Heyzel, the NPC invader in Farron Keep. Then give a pale tongue to the Rosaria's Fingers covenant. Yellowfinger Heyzel's summon sign appears next to the tower full of slugs near the Keep Ruins bonfire. Summon her to receive the gesture. Abyss Watchers must be alive
  • Dignified Bow: Received from meeting Yuria of Londor
  • Duel Bow: Received upon summoning the Londor Pale Shade that appears in various locations when you have five Dark Sigils
  • Legion Etiquette: Pray to the Old Wolf of Farron in the Farron Swamp
  • Darkmoon Loyalty: Received from speaking with Sirris in Firelink Shrine after she arrives
  • By My Sword: Received by summoning Black Hand Gotthard
  • Prayer: Received from Irina of Carim
  • Silent Ally: Given by Orbeck of Vinheim once you buy a certain amount of spells alongside a ring
  • Rest: Received after resting at a bonfire for the first time.
  • Collapse: Received from meeting Hawkwood
  • Patches Squat: Talk to Patches at Firelink Shrine
  • Prostration: Recieved from Patches after replying no when he asks for forgiveness, or after confronting him in the Cathedral of the Deep after he lowers the bridge with "You know who I am"
  • Toast: Received by talking to Siegward of Catarina after assisting him in defeating the demon on the way to the Road of Sacrifices
  • Sleep: Talk to Siegward of Catarina again after learning Toast
  • Curl Up: Recieved from Greirat of the Undead Settlement after giving him Loretta's bone
  • Stretch Out: Found in the Profaned Capital on the corpse next to the first bonfire
  • Path of the Dragon: Received from corpse after Oceiros, the Consumed King
